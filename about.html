Who We Are

Orlando Cyber Security is a cybersecurity and managed IT services company headquartered in Orlando, Florida. We exist for one reason: to protect businesses from the growing wave of cyber threats that target organizations of every size — and to make enterprise-grade security accessible to small and mid-sized companies that need it most.

With over 25 years of combined experience in software development and cybersecurity, our team brings deep technical expertise alongside a practical, business-focused approach. We understand that security is not just a technical issue — it's a business continuity issue. Every hour of downtime, every compromised customer record, and every ransomware payment has a real dollar cost. Our job is to make sure those costs never appear on your balance sheet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading provider of innovative and comprehensive cybersecurity solutions, ensuring the digital safety and resilience of businesses worldwide. We believe that every organization — regardless of size — deserves the same level of protection that Fortune 500 companies receive. We deliver that protection at a price point and service level that works for growing businesses.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all security. Every client engagement begins with understanding your specific business operations, risk tolerance, regulatory environment, and technology stack. From there, we build a security program that fits your needs and scales as you grow.

Our Approach to Cybersecurity

We follow a layered defense model — also called defense in depth. Rather than relying on any single security control, we build multiple overlapping layers of protection so that if one layer fails, others are there to catch the threat. This includes perimeter security (firewalls, email filtering), endpoint protection (EDR, anti-malware), identity controls (MFA, least-privilege access), data protection (encryption, DLP), and continuous monitoring (SIEM, alerting).

We also believe that security is not a project — it's a program. Threats evolve constantly, and so must your defenses. Our managed security clients receive ongoing monitoring, regular assessments, and strategic reviews to ensure their security posture stays ahead of the threat landscape.
