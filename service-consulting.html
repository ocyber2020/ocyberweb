Expert Cybersecurity Consulting Tailored to Your Business

Every business faces unique cybersecurity challenges. Whether you're a healthcare provider navigating HIPAA requirements, a financial services firm managing PCI-DSS compliance, or a growing small business trying to protect customer data, our cybersecurity consulting services deliver actionable, tailored strategies to address your specific risks and regulatory obligations.

  • Comprehensive security risk assessments and gap analysis
  • Security policy and procedure development
  • Regulatory compliance support (HIPAA, PCI-DSS, NIST, SOC 2)
  • Security awareness training programs for employees
  • Incident response planning and tabletop exercises
  • Third-party vendor risk assessment

Our Consulting Process

We begin every engagement with a thorough discovery phase — understanding your business operations, existing security controls, regulatory environment, and risk tolerance. From there, we perform a structured risk assessment that maps your assets, identifies vulnerabilities, and evaluates the likelihood and impact of potential threats.

The output is a clear, prioritized remediation roadmap. We don't just hand you a 200-page report and walk away. We work alongside your team to implement recommendations, build internal security processes, and establish metrics so you can measure progress over time.

Building a Culture of Security

Technical controls are only part of the equation. The majority of successful cyberattacks exploit human error — phishing emails, weak passwords, accidental data exposure. Our consulting engagements include employee security awareness programs that teach your team how to recognize and respond to threats, turning your workforce from a potential vulnerability into your first line of defense.

Whether you need a one-time security assessment or an ongoing advisory relationship, Orlando Cyber Security is here to help you navigate the cybersecurity landscape with confidence.
